Datasets

Datasets are individual chunks of data - either full acquisitions (scenes) or chunks of acquisitions created during ingestion. Information found in the dataset is the id, added date, and acquisition/chunk data such as extents, date acquired, and path to dataset. Please note that the download feature is only available for ingested products.
